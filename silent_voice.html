“Back then, if we could have have heard each other’s voices, everything would have been so much better.” – Shouya Ishida

A silent Voice(2016) is a slice-of-life movie with the story of Ishida Shouya who bullies a deaf girl, Nishimiya Shouko, to the point that she transfers to another school. As a result, he is ostracized and bullied himself with no friends to speak of and no plans for the future. This is the story of his path to redemption.

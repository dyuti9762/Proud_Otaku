My Hero Academa(2016-ongoing) is a Shonen and seasonal anime with the story of Midoriya Izuku who was just a regular middle school student in a world where people with superpowers known as Quirks are the norm. However, he dreams of one day becoming a Hero, despite being bullied by his classmates for not having a Quirk. After being the only one to try and save his childhood friend, Katsuki, from a Villain, All Might, the world's greatest Hero, bestows upon him his own quirk, One For All. The story follows Izuku's entrance into Yuuei High School, a school that cultivates the next generation of Heroes.

    Top 10 Reasons to watch MY_HERO_ACADEMIA
  1. Izuku Midoriya (aka Deku) is a protagonist you want to root for!
  2. The series has a great message.
  3. The Quirks. There is so much versatility in the individual abilities in the show.
  4. The side characters get a chance to strut their stuff.
  5. The FEELS!
  6. The animation during the action sequences is always on point.
  7. The setting is well established and fleshed out in great detail.
  8. Everyone has their own motivations and goals, some of which aren’t exactly ‘noble’.
  9. It’s an origins story (Who doesn’t like a good origin story?!).
  10. The series plays around with a lot of the traditional shonen anime tropes,
    but it never feels like I’m watching the same old shonen anime fodder.
